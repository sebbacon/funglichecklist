Polysporina Vezda, Folia geobot. phytotax. 13: 399 (1978)

Description: Thallus crustose, often inapparent, or on lichens. Photobiont chlorococcoid. Ascomata apothecia, black, perithecia-like, at first with a central pore, later with a thick, radially fissured margin surrounding a circular slit-like disc around a central umbo; old apothecia often gyrose-contorted. Thalline exciple absent. True exciple without algae; outer part dark brown; inner part colourless, of radiating, branched hyphae. Epithecium colourless to brown, but umbo region thick and dark brown. Hymenium colourless, zone below umbo without asci, I+ yellowish to reddish or bluish in parts, K/I+ blue. Hypothecium colourless.
