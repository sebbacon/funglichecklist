Description: Thallus crustose, superficial or more commonly immersed. Prothallus absent. Photobiont chlorocoocoid, including Dictyochloropsis and Myrmecia. True exciple exciple black and friable, becoming excluded or persistent and then in some species crenulate, composed of compacted radiating hyphae, colourless internally, tips swollen and dark brown at the surface.
